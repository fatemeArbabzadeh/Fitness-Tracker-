30-Days Plank Challenge

Overview:

Strengthen your core and build endurance with our 30-Day Plank Challenge! Start small and gradually increase your plank duration each day. Whether you're a beginner or advanced, this challenge is designed to push you to new limits.

Daily Plan:

  • Week 1: Start with a 20-second plank and add 5 seconds each day.
  • Week 2: Increase to 40 seconds, adding 10 seconds each day.
  • Week 3: Aim for a 1-minute plank, increasing by 15 seconds daily.
  • Week 4: Push to 2 minutes, adding 20 seconds each day until you reach 3 minutes.

Tips & Form

  • Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  • Engage your core and avoid sagging your hips.
  • Modify with knee planks if needed.
